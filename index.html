GST Council's 56th Meeting

September 3rd, 2025

Overview & Objectives

The 56th Goods and Services Tax (GST) Council meeting, chaired by the Union Finance & Corporate Affairs Minister, introduced a set of next-generation GST reforms aimed at:

  • Simplifying the tax framework
  • Providing relief to citizens
  • Facilitating trade and commerce
  • Operationalising the Goods and Services Tax Appellate Tribunal (GSTAT)

Reforms include rate rationalisation, process improvements, and enhanced refund mechanisms.
